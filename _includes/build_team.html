Zak Essa

Project Lead

Zak loves to bring people together for the social good and believes that technology can play an important part in bridging the inequalities faced by people living in rural areas. Zak’s role in the centre involved coordinating and collaborating with the local community, Bulungula Incubator, Bulungula College, The Delta and all the exceptional people that were a part of the team. His favourite quote is, “The possibilities are endless when a group of committed, creative, inspired, empowered, engaged leaders go beyond their personal limitations and comfort and form a team to take responsibility for the greater good.”
